About Us

Maadi is a performance & event space with a green roof top lawn, located in central Chennai with easy access from bus & train stations. With a vast air-conditioned hall, Maadi is an ideal place for corporate events, conferences, workshops, trainings & domestic functions such as birthday parties etc. The roof top lawn is an ideal setting to enjoy the beauty of Chennai city during dusk and it is the most sought after for post event photo ops sessions.

Founded by two college friends with a mission to promote art & artists, the pricing is structured to make it very affordable for upcoming artists and aspirational start-ups. To help some deserving artists, we also work on revenue share model as well.

With a vision to build the next generation of artists, we are driven to make every event very successful for our customers. If some of the artists we host goes on to become famous & successful, then we have achieved something on our vision.
